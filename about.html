About Us



Chemplus 2021

Greetings from the Department of Chemical Engineering. In association with Chemical Engineering Society (ChES) and AIChE Students chapter IITM, we are proud to present the 12th annual edition of our technical festival, Chemplus.

Previously being a separate event for undergrads (ChemClave) and postgrads (ChemSymp), Chemplus has transformed into one that incorporates both the fun part in Engineering as well as the research element of a Symposium.

There is no engineering role more versatile and vital than that of a chemical engineer. We at Chemplus celebrate this spirit of inquiry and innovation and emphasize the significant role we play in the creation and manufacturing of materials that are used everyday.

Chemplus provides opportunity to all budding engineers irrespective of their branch of study and graduation status, to compete, exchange ideas, collaborate, learn and of course have fun, all under the same roof. We cordially invite you to take part and learn what it means to be a Universal Engineer.



AICHE

The American Institute of Chemical Engineers (AIChE) is a professional organization, established in 1908 to distinguish Chemical Engineering as a profession independent of Chemists and Mechanical Engineers.

IIT Madras is the second among all the IITs in India part of this prestigious Student Chapter. The student chapters tend to focus on providing networking opportunities in both academia and in industry as well as increasing student involvement locally and nationally.

The IIT Madras Student Chapter of the American Institute of Chemical Engineers (AIChE) is a campus group made up of chemical engineering students. The group's goal is to foster interest in chemical engineering and teach students about the chemical engineering profession.
