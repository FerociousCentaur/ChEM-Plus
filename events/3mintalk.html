ABOUT:

What is Chemical Engineering? What do Non-Chemical Engineering folks know about this field? Guys, here is a chance to introduce what Chemical Engineering actually encompasses. Grab this opportunity to explain Chemical Engineering and its importance in the daily walk of life to the junta. Chemical Engineering society (ChES) in its 14 th edition of ‘CHEM+’ presents CHEM E-TALK (3 minutes talk) where you can surpass your imagination and engineering skills to win some exciting prizes.

Rules:

1) The presentation/audio needs to be delivered in English. No other mode of communication is permitted.
2) Power-point presentation is not mandatory. However it might be invoked for better clarity of explanation.
3) You have to upload your presentation/audio at : Google Form
4) The file size should not exceed 100 MB.
5) Last date for submission is 20/03/2021.
6) The participant can select any topic of their own choice to present.
7) Decision of organizing committee will be final.
8) Maximum number of slides should not exceed 10.
9) The presentation/audio should not exceed 3 minutes. A relaxation of maximum 10 seconds is
permitted. Exceeding of time limit will lead to deduction of score.
