ABOUT:

Do you think you are a professional in Chemical Engineering? Here’s a chance to test your knowledge and general awareness in this field. Chemical Engineering Society (ChES) in its 14 th edition of ‘CHEM+’ presents ‘Quiz Chem+’, a two part quiz featuring various spheres of Chemical Engineering and its connection to the world.

Rules:

1. The Quiz will be conducted online in two rounds: Prelims and Finals.
2. Participation to be done in pairs.
3. Sign in using a proper email ID and mention the names of the members and the name of the College.
4. 20 questions in the prelims on Zoom, with star marked questions to break ties.
5. Top 8 teams make it to the finals, which will happen on Zoom and be recorded.
6. Cheating (using search engines, consulting other teams or collaborating on answers) is prohibited and people found cheating beyond reasonable doubt will be blacklisted from qualifying or participating further in the event.

Can I participate individually in the event?

Yes, however it’s recommended that you participate with another person, since it increases your chance of qualification.

Do we need to sign in through official college email ids?

Yes

How do the prelims work?

A google form is provided at the start of the prelims, with questions appearing on the Zoom link, which you’ll fill in as a team as and when the questions come up, and you’ll submit the form by the end of the prelims. Finalists will be intimated after results are compiled, and the finals will be on another zoom link, but with live answering.
