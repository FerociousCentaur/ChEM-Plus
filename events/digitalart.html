ABOUT:

Are you a Chemical Engineering Professional? Here is an opportunity to demonstrate your know-how in the world of chemical technology. The digital art contest, a competition for digital architecture in the different realms of chemical engineering and links to the environment are posed by the Chemical Engineering Society (ChES) in its 14th edition of 'CHEM+'.

Rules:

1. Participation can be done in teams as well as individually.
2. The decision of the jury committee/judges will be final.
3. Cheating (using search engines, consulting other teams or working on responses) is forbidden and persons found to cheat would be blacklisted from competing or participating further in the event beyond a reasonable doubt.
4. The art has to be uploaded at: Google Form
5. The last date for submission is 20.03.2021.
6. The art itself should depict the theme of the problem statement.
